Description

Appointos (hereinafter: Application) is a piece of software created to To book appointments - and customized for Apple mobile devices. It is used to Online appointment.


Manage your business

Streamline your business by managing your schedule with an all-in-one booking solution. With 24/7 online booking capability, appointments can be scheduled after hours - when clients prefer to book most. Reduce no-shows and reschedule or cancel appointments in a jiffy.


Online Scheduling

With our scheduling application, clients can easily view your availability and self-schedule their own appointments. As it is a 24/7 online booking service , clients can book from anywhere and on anytime which boosts your productivity


Organize your schedule

We send automated notification when clients reschedule or cancel their appointments by themselves. This way you track all the session on point.


Store client information

All your clients details will be stored in a secured place where you can always lookup them in the application and book appointments in a few taps.


Send Reminders

Automated reminders are sent to clients via text and email. It helps them to plan ahead. This also could reduce no shows and prevent last minute cancellations.
